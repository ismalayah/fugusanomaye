Vernon Hill Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Vernon W. Hill II (born August 18, 1945) is an American businessman who is the founder and chairman of Metro Bank, a UK retail bank with 20 locations and assets of £1.2 billion ($2.0 billion). He was born in New York City. He is also the founder, former Chairman, President, and CEO of Commerce Bancorp, based in Cherry Hill, New Jersey, as well as the Chairman of Fetch Inc., a technology company (aka Pet Plan North America).
