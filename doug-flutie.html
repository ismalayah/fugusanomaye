Who is Doug Flutie:

Doug Flutie is a famous Football Player. He was born on October 23, 1962 and his birthplace is Manchester, MD. Doug is also well known as, Former quarterback who threw a remarkable game-winning hail mary touchdown for Boston College against Miami in 1984. He spent his NFL career with four different teams: the Bears, Patriots, Bills and Chargers.

Doug is originated from United States.  He became a competitor on season 22 of ABC’s Dancing With the Stars alongside pro Karina Smirnoff.
