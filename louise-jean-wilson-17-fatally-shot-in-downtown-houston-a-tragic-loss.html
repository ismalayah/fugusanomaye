Louise Jean Wilson, 17, Fatally Shot in Downtown Houston: A Tragic Loss

It is with profound sadness that we report the tragic passing of 17-year-old Louise Jean Wilson from Whitney, Texas. Louise was fatally shot while traveling through downtown Houston en route to Galveston.

The incident took place on December 10, 2023, and authorities are investigating it as a road rage shooting. According to reports, the shooter opened fire after Louise allegedly cut off another vehicle on I-45.

Louise’s family members have confirmed her identity and are grieving this untimely loss. Known for her vibrant personality and kind heart, Louise’s passing has left her community in shock and mourning.

In response to the tragedy, a GoFundMe campaign has been set up to support Louise’s family during this challenging time. The campaign aims to assist with funeral expenses and provide some financial relief for the family as they navigate this heartbreaking situation.

As this story continues to unfold, our thoughts go out to Louise’s family, friends, and the entire Whitney community. May they find strength and solace during this difficult time.
