WWE NXT 2.0 Results (5/10/22)

Amari Miller vs. Alba Fyre

Miller fires up and lands a leaping neck breaker. Fyre kicks out. Miller tries a running elbow but Fyre moves out of the way. Fyre lands a superkick followed by a tornado DDT. Basement superkick by Fyre. Miller kicks out. Miller escapes the Fyre Bomb and lands a PK. Fyre kicks out. Fyre lands the Fyre Bomb. Fyre follows that with a swanton for the win.

Winner- Alba Fyre

Andre Chase and Sarray w/Bodhi Hayward vs. Grayson Waller and Tiffany Stratton

Waller cuts Chase’s flurry of offense off by going low. Chase starts to fire up, but Waller tags in Stratton. Sarray chases Stratton around the ring. Sarray eventually traps Stratton in the Muta Lock. Stratton pulls Sarray’s hair to break the hold. Waller tries a sneak attack but Chase cuts him off. Sarray and Chase land stereo stomps while spelling Chase U. After the break, Waller bows up at Sarray. Chase grabs Waller and holds him in place so Sarray can obliterate him with the Sunray dropkick. Sarray pins Stratton after a distraction from Hayward.

Winners- Andre Chase and Sarray

Lyons pins Grace after a spin kick.

Winner- Nikkita Lyons

Cora Jade vs. Natalya

Jade takes an early advantage. Natalya cuts her off and works her over to set up the sharpshooter. Jade fires up and locks Natalya in the Sharpshooter. Natalya escapes and traps Jade in the Sharpshooter. Jade tries to get to the ropes but Natalya pulls her back to the middle of the ring. Jade passes out.

Winner- Natalya

After the match, Natalya helps Jade up and they hug.
