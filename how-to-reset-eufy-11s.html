How do I reconnect my EUFY vacuum to WiFi?

How do I reset my EUFY doorbell?

The reset button on Eufy video doorbell 2K (wireless) is the SYNC button located on the rear side of the doorbell. To factory reset the doorbell, press and hold the SYNC button for 10 seconds. To soft reset the doorbell, quick-press the SYNC button 5 times in 3 seconds to power off the doorbell.

Why is my EUFY light red?

When RoboVac is in trouble, it will emit a series of short or long beeps and the red indicator on RoboVac will be on or flashing. Why is my EUFY going in circles?

If the plastic over the sensors is too badly scuffed or dirty, the light from the sensor scatters off of the plastic and constantly tells the unit that there is an obstacle. So it spins around looking for someplace that it can go without hitting something, and fails.
